FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How long does delivery take? +
Standard service guarantees delivery within 24 hours of collection. Our Express service returns your clothes within 6 hours for urgent needs. We also offer same-day service for orders placed before 9 AM.
How is pricing calculated? +
Pricing is based on the service type and bag weight. Basic Wash & Fold starts at $10 per bag. Dry cleaning items are priced per garment. You always see the full price estimate before confirming your booking — no surprises.
What if an item is lost or damaged? +
All items in our care are fully insured. If anything is lost or damaged due to our process, we compensate at full replacement value. We photograph items at collection and return for complete transparency.
Which areas do you serve? +
We currently serve the downtown core and surrounding suburbs within a 15-mile radius. Enter your postcode during booking to instantly confirm coverage. We're expanding to new areas every month.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Payment is only charged after your order is confirmed — never upfront.
Do you use eco-friendly products? +
Yes! All our detergents are biodegradable and hypoallergenic. We offer a fragrance-free option for customers with sensitive skin or allergies. Our facilities are powered by 100% renewable energy.
